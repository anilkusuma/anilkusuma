Crown E-Learner App

     Android App for Crown E-Learner,It helps students to learn anywhere through digital content, no more books or laptops. It also facilitates parents to monitor childs activities sitting in the office, like track student bus location, monitor the attendance after each class, pay fees anytime, go through the student dairy or upcomming exams or exam resutls and much more.It supports all features web app supports, like sending attachements, uploading to gallery etc.
