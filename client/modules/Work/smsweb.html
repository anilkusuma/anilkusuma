Crown E-Learner

     Crown E-Learner is a cloud base ERP Solution for schools. It offers everything a school needs under one platform. Major features are Messaging(along with attachments), Bulk Sms and Notifications, Online Fee Payment, School Galary, Student Blogs, Digital E-Learning content, Online Assessments, Exams, Reports, Transport Management etc.

     Complete Application is designed following material guidelines and componets. Every feature is handcrafted with great care.

     Demo Contains a school with 1 Admin, 5 Classes, 10 Sections,100 Students(Each section 10 students) and 20 Teachers. Explore the demo, create new students,teachers, login with those students, exchanges mails between them,upload images, create fees etc.
