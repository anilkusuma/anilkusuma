Vehicle Tracking Software

     Vehicle Tracking Software is used by fleet owners to easily manage their fleet. It provides accurate location of the vehicle in real time and complete history of the vehicle route. Various alerts like over speeding, geo-fences, fuel removal, Idle time etc will notified to owner in real time. It performs various analysis on the data received from the terminal and create variety of reports in tabular format. A dashboard is provided to owner, on which an overview of complete fleet is displayed in very intuitive way, by using graphical and pictorial representation.

     Application is designed in such a way that, it can be offered to other similar companies in the field, Admin user of that company can add customers under his company and manage his customers independently.
