Hotel Management Software

     A Freelance project for a Hotel Owner. The basic requirement of the application is to help the Hotel owner to monitor, check-ins and checkouts of the hotel. Owner(Admin) will create, Rooms and their details, hotel specific configurations, Reception users. At the time of new booking Reception should enter the guest details in the application which will be notified to owner, once room is booked, admin should approve any changes like cancellation,extension or checkout for that booking. With out admin approval room won't be available for next booking.

     Along with this basic requirement, we have provided the client a dashboard, which a very comprehensive status of hotel bookings. Graphical representation of analytics like most canceled rooms, room with heighest bookings, monthly & weekly sale graphs, demands etc.
